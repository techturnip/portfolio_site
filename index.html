About Me

My name is Tyler Turnipseed and I am a full stack web developer. I began my code journey when I was thirteen years old, learning HTML and coding my first website with Angelfire. Five years ago I realized that web development is more than just a hobby for me.

I began taking courses on Udemy for Angular, React, and WordPress. When I discovered Lambda School I enrolled immediately, and for the past year I've learned how to develop web applications from the front to the back. Now I can proudly say, I am ready and eager to start my web development career!
